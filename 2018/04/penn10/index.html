Penn10

Penn 10 is a collection of student profiles spotlighting members of the senior class that have done incredible work during their time here. Earlier this month, we asked you to nominate seniors who embody innovation and excellence. In turn we got 10 students from all corners of Penn, in the lab, on the court, and at the protests. More importantly, we picked students with real stories and real things to say. And now, it’s your turn to get to know them.
