Best Vegan Food

Magic Carpet

Your
Pick

Not only one of Penn’s favorite food trucks, Magic Carpet also wins best vegan spot on campus. Offering a plethora of hearty, vegan dishes including tofu meatballs, veggie chili, and spicy seitan pepper–steak, Magic Carpet skips the meat without sacrificing flavor.

36th and Spruce Streets, southwest corner and 34th and Walnut Streets, southwest corner
